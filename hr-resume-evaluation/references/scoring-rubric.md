# Scoring Rubric

The score is a review aid, not a hiring decision.

## Score Basis

- `resume_plus_jd_company`: total score is resume quality out of 60 plus JD/company match out of 40.
- `resume_quality_only`: no JD/company context was evaluated; total score is normalized from the resume-quality dimensions to 100.

Batch summaries must show score basis and must not compare the two bases as equivalent.

## Resume Quality, 60 Points

| Dimension | Points | Evidence |
|---|---:|---|
| information_completeness | 10 | Education, experience, projects, skills, achievements, contact details. |
| structure_readability | 10 | Clear hierarchy, readable density, concise wording, ATS-friendly structure. |
| experience_credibility | 10 | Coherent timeline, specific ownership, responsibilities consistent with results. |
| achievement_impact | 15 | Business or technical outcomes, scale, ranking, efficiency, quality, revenue, users, customers. |
| capability_clarity | 10 | Specific skills, personal contribution, concrete tools or methods. |
| risk_review_signals | 5 | Contradictions, vague claims, missing context, sensitive information, manual review items. |

## JD and Company Match, 40 Points

| Dimension | Points | Evidence |
|---|---:|---|
| hard_requirement_match | 10 | Visible match with job-related hard requirements. Gaps require manual review, not automatic rejection. |
| core_capability_match | 12 | Match to JD duties and required capabilities. |
| domain_industry_relevance | 6 | Relevant business domain, customer type, product form, technical stack, or industry context. |
| seniority_match | 6 | Ownership, complexity, leadership, influence, delivery scope. |
| company_team_preference_match | 4 | Match to explicit company/team context. |
| interview_validation_value | 2 | Produces useful verification questions. |
