# Outreach Guidelines

- Use a complete public professional email only.
- Explain why the candidate is being contacted and identify the relevant paper.
- Describe only approved company, team, role and visit information.
- Make declining future contact simple.
- Create one personalized draft per person; merge related roles into one coherent message.
- Do not batch-send or switch to another address after delivery failure.
- Follow up only on day 7 and day 21 from the actual initial send time.
- Cancel unsent follow-ups after any reply.
- Recheck approval, identity, prior contact and suppression immediately before send.
