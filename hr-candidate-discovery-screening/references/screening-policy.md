# Screening Policy

## Allowed decisions

- Determine whether the current public institution is in mainland China.
- Classify public current status as doctoral student, master's student, undergraduate, postdoc, faculty, company employee, other or unknown.
- Compare verified education status with job hard and preferred requirements.
- Explain paper-to-JD relevance with cited evidence.

## Prohibited inference

Do not collect or infer nationality, household registration, ethnicity, age, sex, marriage, pregnancy, health, religion or other protected or irrelevant characteristics.

Do not merge same-name authors without a strong identifier or sufficient corroborating evidence. Conflicts and missing evidence go to human review.

Use automation only for recommendations. A human makes the final outreach and recruitment decision.
